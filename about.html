About MindMuscleCollective.com

Norman Cheung founded MindMuscleCollective with thinking fitness enthusiasts in mind; individuals who are passionate about training as well as nutrition, lifestyle, health and general self development. The goal of MindMuscleCollective is to explore the ever expanding science of training and health by blogging a regular stream of useful ideas that is evidence-based.


About Strength Coach Norman Cheung

(Chun Wun Norman Cheung)

As a Theoretical Physics graduate from UCL, he has developed a habit of having a scientific approach to his work. He recognises that there is a need for having online information that is reliable and research supported, as well as being easy to read and understand.

Norman has been incredibly passionate about strength sports for over 6 years. He has been coaching powerlifters and teaching weightlifting at UCLU Barbell Club (formerly UCLU Weightlifting Club) since its founding and QM Weightlifting Powerlifting since 2015, which are university sports clubs at University College London and Queen Mary University of London respectively.

He has worked with numerous powerlifters who frequently medal nationally, 2 of whom eventually went on to compete internationally and a few who are currently holding national records in the GBPF.

Along with coaching powerlifters at both clubs, he also runs (a busy) online powerlifting coaching service for lifters in drug tested federations particularly GBPF and has lifters spread across the UK and Personal Training at Qmotion Health and Fitness Centre at Queen Mary University of London, in East London.

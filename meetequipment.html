The Big List of Competition Equipment

by Chun Wun Norman Cheung

Posted on August 24, 2013 at 9:00 PM

Equipment Checklist:
  • Membership Card
  • Singlet/Costume
  • Underwear
  • Knee Sleeves
  • Wrist Wraps
  • Powerlifting Belt
  • Weightlifting Shoes
  • Deadlift Socks
  • Deadlift Footwear
  • Camcorder/Camera/Camera Phone
  • MP3 + Headphones
  • Talcum/Baby Powder
  • Chalk (just in case)
  • Warm Up Tracksuits and Jumper
  • Towel
  • Cash
  • Thumb Tape (Optional)
  • Smelling Salts (Optional)
  • Balm (Optional)
  • Ibuprofen (Optional)
  • Mobility Tools: Foam Roller, Lacrosse Ball, Resistance Bands
  • Change of Clothes
  • Pen and Paper/Journal
Food and Drink Checklist:
  • Water (Loads)
  • Electrolyte Drinks (Coconut Water, Lucozade or other Electrolyte Drinks)
  • Fruit
  • Caffeine Source (and L-theanine - high recommend to take the anxiety edge of caffeine)
  • Intra-Workout Carb Drink
  • Calorie Dense Food that is Light on Stomach
  • Protein Powder
  • Sandwiches
  • BCAA Powder
  • Plasters
